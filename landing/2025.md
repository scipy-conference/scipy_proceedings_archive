The 24th annual SciPy conference was held in Tacoma, WA at the Tacoma Convention Center, July 7-13, 2025.

SciPy brings together attendees from industry, academia and government to showcase their latest projects, learn from skilled users and developers, and collaborate on code development.

Full proceedings, posters and slides, and organizing committee can be found at https://proceedings.scipy.org/articles/proceedings-2025
